3. **量子临界点的非可逆对称性**

   [Conditional probability density functional theory for solids](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13226)

### **实验进展**
1. **石墨烯纳米带的电化学直写**  
   [Direct-write electrochemical nanofabrication of ultrasmall graphene devices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12660)

2. **超导二极管的场无关操作**  
   [In-situ tunable superconducting diode](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13254)

3. **拓扑声子分子与链的实验实现**  
   [Coupled Topological Interface States and Phonon Molecules in GaAs/AlAs Superlattices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12635)  
   在GaAs/AlAs超晶格中，通过串联拓扑超晶格实现了耦合拓扑界面态，形成可调谐的声子分子链。实验通过瞬态反射率测量验证了GHz频段的拓扑声子架构。
