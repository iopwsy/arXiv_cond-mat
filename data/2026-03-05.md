今日arXiv发布了大量凝聚态物理（CMP）相关论文（约100余篇），涵盖拓扑物态、超导、磁性、自旋电子学、量子材料、计算材料科学等领域。以下按**新的理论进展**、**计算进展**和**实验进展**分类总结重点论文，每类选取5-8篇代表性工作，突出创新点，并附arXiv链接。总结基于摘要和标题筛选，聚焦原创贡献。

#### 1. **理论进展**（新模型、相变、拓扑等）
- **[Symmetry-protected topology and deconfined solitons in a multi-link Z₂ gauge theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03374)** (Domanti et al.): 提出多链Z₂格点规范理论中对称保护拓扑与解禁闭独子，揭示Peierls不稳定性类似现象及电场诱导的分数量子化。理论基于矩阵乘积态分析，预测拓扑独子对。
- **[Quantum Theory of Functionally Graded Materials](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03424)** (Landry et al.): 发展功能梯度材料的从头量子理论，引入调制Bloch态和广义WKB方法，揭示梯度介质中有效张量失效及Landau量子化控制。应用于p-n结。
- **[Mixed-state Phases from Higher-order SSPTs with Kramers-Wannier Symmetry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03455)** (Mana et al.): 混合态相由高阶子系统SPT相迹化产生，揭示对称保护拓扑序与强-弱对称破缺共存，利用界面探针表征。
- **[Electrostatically-induced topological phase transitions in polyacetylene molecules](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03473)** (Suleiman et al.): 栅极电压诱导聚乙炔分子拓扑相变，玻色化框架下多Kink解描述电荷密度与二聚化域壁。
- **[A Fermi Surface Driven Spiral Spin Liquid](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03434)** (Neves et al.): 费米面驱动的螺旋自旋液，漫散射揭示准2D空穴袋介导的磁相互作用，Monte Carlo模拟相图。
- **[Symmetry selection rules for the intrinsic nonlinear thermal Hall effect in altermagnets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03599)** (Kim): 交替磁体中量子度规驱动的非线性热Hall效应对称选择规则，需C₂旋转对称破缺。
- **[Dynamical Superfluid and Bose-Insulator Phases in Quantized Polariton Lattices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03600)** (Ghosh): 量子化极化子晶格中动态超流与Bose绝缘相，非线性转移导致相扩散抑制相干。

#### 2. **计算进展**（DFT、ML、模拟等）
- **[TritonDFT: Automating DFT with a Multi-Agent Framework](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03372)** (Hu et al.): 多代理框架自动化DFT工作流，Pareto优化精度-成本，支持DFTBench基准。开源：https://github.com/Leo9660/TritonDFT。
- **[Computational discovery of bifunctional organic semiconductors for energy and biosensing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03392)** (Kongo et al.): 高通量筛选PubChemQC数据集，PCESAScore度量发现PCE达36.1%的多功能有机半导体。
- **[Predicting Spin-Crossover Behavior in Metal-Organic Frameworks from Limited and Noisy Data Using Quantile Active Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03545)** (Jose et al.): 分位数主动学习从噪声数据预测MOF自旋交叉，恢复82%真阳性，发现pSCO-105候选。
- **[Coupled-cluster approach to vibronic effects in resonant inelastic x-ray scattering of quantum materials](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03551)** (Matsuzaki et al.): EOM-CC方法模拟Re L₃边RIXS谱，揭示T_{2g}振动耦合肩峰。
- **[Enhanced superconductivity in palladium hydrides by non-perturbative electron-phonon effects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03492)** (Bianco, Errea): 非微扰电子-声子耦合提升PdH/D超导Tc，恢复反常同位素效应。
- **[Strain effects on n-type doping in AlN](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03528)** (Wang, Van de Walle): 张力工程降低AlN施主电离能，Si_{Al} DX中心从271meV降至98meV。
- **[Machine learning assisted High-Throughput study of M₄X₃Tₓ MXenes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04103)** (Goel, Kashyap): ML高通量DFT研究234种MXenes，分类磁性及稳定性。
- **[Dispersion and lifetimes of magnons in non-collinear magnets from time dependent density functional theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04111)** (Eilmsteiner et al.): TDDFT计算非共线磁体磁子色散及Landau阻尼。

#### 3. **实验进展**（新材料、测量、器件等）
- **[Limited coincidence between ultrahigh-field superconductivity and line of metamagnetic endpoints in UTe₂](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03500)** (Czajka et al.): UTe₂超高场超导与元磁端点线不重合，ab平面窄角区内超导延伸至73T。
- **[Kondo driven suppression of charge density wave in Van der Waals material UTe₃](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03509)** (Shotton et al.): UTe₃中Kondo杂化抑制CDW，ARPES揭示费米面嵌套但无CDW。
- **[Probing Interface-Driven Mechanisms of Non-Classical Light in van der Waals Heterostructures](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03534)** (Rosa et al.): WSe₂/hBN/Clinochlore异质结构中界面介导单光子发射，g^{(2)}(0)=0.13，强度增强5倍。
- **[Graphene Zero-Bias Sub-Terahertz Turnkey Detector with Above 43 GHz Bandwidth](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03554)** (Titova et al.): 石墨烯天线耦合亚THz探测器，带宽>43GHz，零偏压。
- **[Magnetic Signature of Chiral Phonons Revealed by Neutron Spectroscopy in Ferrimagnetic Fe₁.₇₅Zn₀.₂₅Mo₃O₈](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03635)** (Bao et al.): 中子谱揭示铁磁体中手性声子磁信号，磁子-声子强耦合。
- **[Plasmonic polaron in self-intercalated 1T-TiS₂](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03663)** (Choi et al.): ARPES/HR-EELS观察自镶嵌1T-TiS₂中等离子体极化子，调谐自旋密度波。
- **[X-ray magnetic circular dichroism evidence of intrinsic d-wave altermagnetism in rutile-structure NiF₂](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03694)** (Li et al.): XMCD证实金红石NiF₂ d波交替磁性，自旋倾斜诱导弱铁磁。
- **[NMR evidence of spin supersolid and Pomeranchuk effect behaviors in the triangular-lattice antiferromagnet Rb₂Ni₂(SeO₃)₃](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03703)** (Chen et al.): NMR观察三角格点Rb₂Ni₂(SeO₃)₃自旋超固与Pomeranchuk效应。
