#### **理论进展**（新机制与相图探索）

- **[3] Interaction-Driven Ferrimagnetic Stripes in the Extended Hubbard Model** ([arXiv:2603.12309](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12309))：扩展Hubbard模型中最近邻排斥V/U>0.25诱导铁磁条纹相，与反铁磁/电荷密度波纠缠。QMC和DMRG模拟显示次近邻跳跃t'改变调制波长。
- **[4] Fracton Spin Liquid and Exotic Frustrated Phases in Ising-like Octochlore Magnets** ([arXiv:2603.12313](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12313))：八面体晶格（octochlore）上Ising自旋的相图，包括自旋冰、fracton自旋液体（X-cube模拟）和自旋向列相。预测反钙钛矿和氟化钾化合物实现。
- **[7] Parity and time-reversal invariant Ising spin ordering** ([arXiv:2603.12330](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12330))：引入共面反铁磁体产生实空间Ising自旋序，保持P和T对称。预测纵向/横向自旋电导、非相对论altermagnetic/奇偶自旋分裂。
- **[9] Zero-field superconducting vortices and Majorana zero modes pinned by magnetic islands in correlated Rashba systems** ([arXiv:2603.12338](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12338))：磁岛诱导零场涡旋和Majorana零模，在无长程磁序的Rashba超导体中。预测拓扑绝缘体表面和平面Rashba超导实现。
- **[26] Ferroaxial magnets: time-reversal-even mirror symmetry violation from spin order** ([arXiv:2603.12502](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12502))：自旋序诱导铁轴极化（ferroaxial），保持T和反演对称。预测非相对论多铁性和三阶非线性霍尔效应。
- **[32] Linear Magnetoresistance as a Probe of the Neel Vector in Altermagnets with Vanishing Anomalous Hall Effect** ([arXiv:2603.12692](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12692))：线性磁电阻探测altermagnets中Néel矢量，即使异常霍尔效应消失。CrSb第一性原理验证。
- **[47] Finite-momentum superconductivity with singlet-triplet mixing in an altermagnetic metal** ([arXiv:2603.12897](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12897))：altermagnetic金属中有限动量超导，单重-三重混合。预测d_xy和d_{x^2-y^2}波altermagnet相图。
- **[59] Extending Topological Bound on Quantum Weight Beyond Symmetry-Protected Topological Phases** ([arXiv:2603.13041](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13041))：拓扑量子重量下界扩展至对称破缺系统，通过投影谱拓扑不变量。
- **[63] SU(2) gauge theory of fluctuating stripe order in the two-dimensional Hubbard model** ([arXiv:2603.13071](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13071))：SU(2)规范理论描述Hubbard模型中波动条纹序，产生费米弧和自旋隙伪隙相。
